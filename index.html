About

Where Discipline Meets Conviction

Dr.Hash Consulting was founded in 2021 with a singular thesis: the best financial decisions emerge at the intersection of quantitative rigor and deep market intuition. What began as a research-focused crypto advisory has evolved into a multi-vertical financial intelligence firm serving clients across Asia, North America, and Europe.

Our founder Wesley brings an unusual combination of skills — a professional poker career that instilled discipline in probabilistic thinking and bankroll management, combined with a quantitative trading background that demands systematic execution. This dual lens shapes every aspect of our advisory practice.

We don't chase narratives. We build models, test hypotheses, and let data guide our convictions. When we're wrong, we cut fast. When we're right, we size appropriately. This philosophy has sustained us through multiple market cycles and earned the trust of thousands of clients.

Our Approach

Principles Over Predictions

Markets reward process, not prophecy. Our methodology is built on three pillars proven resilient across bull and bear cycles.

Quantitative Foundation

Every thesis starts with data. Proprietary databases covering on-chain metrics, exchange flows, derivatives positioning, social sentiment, and macroeconomic indicators. Models are backtested across multiple market regimes before deployment.

Risk-First Architecture

Position sizing, correlation management, and downside protection are the foundation. Drawing from poker's bankroll management — never risk more than the edge justifies. Capital preservation enables compounding.

Transparent Execution

All positions documented with entry rationale, risk parameters, and exit criteria. Regular performance attribution reports. Accountability builds trust; trust enables long-term partnerships.
